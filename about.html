About LeadWell

Your guide for thriving through disruption

Andrea J. Miller has spent 20+ years helping professionals, leaders, and organizations navigate major disruptions, from technology transformations to career pivots to global transitions. She's guided clients across 50+ countries through complex changes that actually stick.

Here's what makes her different: she's lived this journey herself. From technophobe to AI adopter, from traditional career paths to reinvention, from international development to supporting others through disruption, she understands what it feels like to navigate uncertainty without all the answers.

A background built for complex times

Andrea's career spans some of the world's most demanding environments, from complex, multinational UN agencies to Fortune 500 healthcare technology companies, to health tech startups navigating rapid innovation. She's managed multi-million dollar budgets, advised C-suite leaders, and co-authored internationally published policy papers distributed in over 100 countries.

Organizations she's worked with include: Siemens Healthineers, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), World Health Organization (WHO), UN High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR), UNAIDS, UN Women, UNICEF, plus dozens of startups and mid-size organizations across healthcare, technology, finance, and social impact sectors.

Why Her Approach Works

Deep expertise across the human and technical dimensions of change

Most experts specialize in one narrow area. Andrea brings something rare: fluency across both sides of disruption. She holds certifications in emotional intelligence (EQ), adaptability (AQ), and responsible AI leadership, combined with a master's degree in international and intercultural management and executive training in social entrepreneurship from the University of Pennsylvania.

This unique combination means she understands not just the technical challenges of AI adoption or the psychological barriers to change, she understands how they intersect in real organizations with real people from diverse cultural backgrounds.
